# LifeOS AI

### AI-Powered Personal Operating System

LifeOS AI is an intelligent personal operating system designed to combine goals, productivity, knowledge management, decision support, routines, analytics, and AI assistance into one unified ecosystem.

---

## Overview

People use dozens of disconnected tools to manage their goals, tasks, knowledge, habits, notes, decisions, and personal growth.

LifeOS AI aims to create a centralized intelligence layer that helps individuals organize information, optimize routines, make better decisions, and continuously improve performance.

---

## Problem Statement

Modern personal productivity systems often suffer from:

- fragmented information
- disconnected tools
- poor knowledge retrieval
- weak decision support
- lack of long-term visibility
- inefficient goal tracking
- manual planning processes
- limited personal analytics

---

## Proposed Solution

LifeOS AI is designed to:

- centralize personal information
- manage goals and routines
- organize knowledge
- support decision-making
- automate productivity workflows
- provide personal analytics
- create an intelligent life management ecosystem
